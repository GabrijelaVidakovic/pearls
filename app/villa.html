Your home away from home

East Pearl House

13 ROOMS FOR 14 PEOPLE, 18 BATHROOMS, PRIVATE BEACH NEXT TO THE VILLA

Discretely located directly on the silky sands of world renowned Long Bay beach in Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Islands, The Pearls are marvelous beachfront homes that offer unforgettable views of the azure blue Atlantic. These stunning 8 bed 8.5 bath beachfront villas marry the essence of the Caribbean with extreme luxury, resulting in 12000 square feet each of simple splendor, sophistication and seclusion. Whether visiting for a romantic getaway or to escape the real world with your family and friends, welcome to the magical world of The Pearls of Long Bay Estate.
